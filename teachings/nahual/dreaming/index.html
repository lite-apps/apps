  • Quetzaltzin - Recapitulation

    • Becoming a Dreamer


    • Repetitions: Daily for 30-45mins. for 36 days in a row | Time Period: Anytime |
      1. Stand in front of mirror with long nose mask (tonal mask).
      2. Narrate the story of your life. Speak out loud about your problems, worries, etc. as if you were telling a therapist. For 30-45 mins. everyday for 36 days.
      3. This process will unite the tonal and nahual, the conscious and unconscious.
      4. This will enable you to see your self as the actor instead of only being the spectator and you will detach your personal history from your face. The story of your life will stop affecting you and you will be free.
      5. You can change masks mid exercise if it feels right.
