    The 7 Centers

  • The 7 Caves of Power

    • This cave empowers the hands for healing. This cave means 'five moons' in reference to the 5 lunar phases.
      Exercise: Summon the Power of the Moon
      1. Determine what the current moon phase is of the 5.
      2. Go outside and point the corresponding lunar finger up for 9 minutes while calling Metztli Xihualhi
      3. For the Dark Moon point up the thumb and ask to allow you to create whatever you wish.
      For the Waxing Moon point up the index finger and ask for the power to make your creations grow.
      For the full moon point up the middle finger and ask to make your request & healings blossom.
      For the Waning Moon point up the ring finger and ask to reduce your problems and if appropriate your patients problems.
      For the New Moon point up the pinky finger and ask the moon to create new cycles in your life and your patient's lives.
      Ometeotl.

    • This cave means 'the cave of the game' in other words 'pleasure'. The energy that gives you pleasure is what you use for healing e.g. food, sex, entertainment pleasure energy is what you use to heal.
      Exercise: Turn Pleasure Into Power
      1. Put your hands in the shape of a bowl in front of your Ihuitl energy center, the sexual area.
      2. Call the pleasure that certain actions for example eating or sex bring you to come into your hands by saying 'Xihualhui'
      3. When you feel the enery arrive, lift your hands to your throat, the Topilli energy center in your neck.
      4. Command that the pleasure you are holding transforms into healing power.
      5. When finished put your hands together and say 'Ometeotl'

    • This is 'the cave of the feather of altered consciousness' which allows you to enter (or drink by the ancients) the state of dreaming while awake. This altered state allows you to alter reality just as you can in lucid dreams. Here we merge the tonal and the nahual.
      Exercise: The Feather
      1. On the day of the new moon write a message to Metztli the moon and ask here to bring you a feather for dreaming while awake.
      2. Burn your message and blow the ashes to the moon
      3. The next feather that you find or that someone gives you as a present will be from the bird that will become your ally and help you reach this altered state of consciousness.
